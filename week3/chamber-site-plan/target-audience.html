Web Site Audience

Target Audience

The target audience for WDD Chamber of Commerce includes the following:

Who: Citizens in the local areas, travelers coming to the areas, farmers, school children, parents, and others trying to plan activities in or learn more about the area

Age: 8-100

Income: $30,000 and up annually

Education: 4th grade and up

Technology: Personal computing devices, including, but not limited to, cell phones, tablets, and personal computers
